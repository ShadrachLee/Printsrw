Reka duce urubanza(Niba) / IF

Niba Umupira uguciye hejuru wigirayo. Niba ubonye umwubo imbere yawe urasimbuka.
Iyo dushaka ko mudasobwa(computer) ica urubanza(ikora ikintu runaka bitewe n'ikindi) dukoresha iri tegeko "IF(Niba)"
Niba buto yo kuri IchigoJam utayikanze, Porogaramu irisubiramo kumurongo wa 10 gutyo gutyo.
Niba ukanze iyo buto porogaramu irahita ijya ahakurikira hanyuma irangire(nbese ihagarare).
Igihe cyose ukanze buto Agatara kazaka guhite kazima(kumyasa).
Yewee, Mugihe ukanze buto itari riraka!
Ariko niba mudasobwa yawe yihuta, uzakanda kuri buto itara ryake hanyuma ihite ikomeza kurindi tegeko(urubanza) ako kanya.
Niba yanze kujya kurinditegeko ako kanya, ongeramo "WAIT".
Gerageza gukora ibi:
1. Niba wakanze buto, kora ku buryo mudasobwa ikomeza kumva ko ugikanze kuri buto nubwo waba warekuye.
2. Gera geza guhindura uva kuri "WIAT30" ujya kuri "WIAT60"
3. Gera geza guhindura uva kuri "WIAT30" ujya kuri "WIAT60"
4. Hindura "IF BTN()=1"
5. Kora porogaramu uzajya ukanda buto, itara rigatangira ryaka rimyasa(rizima ryaka).
6. Kora porogaramu ituma itara ryishimaa! Make a program that operates the LED happily
