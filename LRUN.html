Huza amaporogaramu / LRUN - IchigoJam

IchigoJam ishobora kubika porogaramu zigera kuri enye(4).
Huza izo porogaramu, kuburyo zikorera hamwe(ziba nkaho ari porogaramu imwe)!
Bwa mbere na mbere, Kora porogaramu y'umukino wo kubazwa, Igice kingenzi cy'umukino.
Izerekana "Clear" mugihe igisubizo aricyo. naho mugihe igisubizo ataricyo bizaba "Game over".
Bika iyi porogaramu mu bubiko bwa mbere(1).
Kugirango uhitemo umutwe wa porogaramu, mbere nambere hera hejuru(mbese kuntangiriro).
Umutwe waprogogaramu uzavamo(uzarangira) mugihe ugize buto ukanda yose.
Ongeraho itegeko rihuza iro porogaramu zombi ari ryo "LRUN".
Duhuje umukino hamwe n'umutwe wa porogaramu!
Gerageza!
1. Tangiza umukino mugihe haba kurangira kwawo hamwe na "LRUN0"
2. Hinduta umutwe wa porogaramu
3. Hindura ikibazo(ibazwa)
4. Kora kuburyo bisubira ku mutwe mugihe umukino urangiye(Game over)
5. Kumukino wa "Kawakudari Game" Shyiramo umutwe(titile).
