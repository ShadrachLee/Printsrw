Murika! / LED - IchigoJam

Fungura aka buto kari kuri IchigoJam. Muri sikirini Andika "LED1" wifashishije icyandikishwa(Keyboard). Hanyuma kanda buto ya "ENTER". (Buto ya "ENTER" iherereye iburyo bwawe kuri keyboard, Nkuko uyi bona kuri iyo foto).
Niba ubona urumuri byakunze rwose kandi urabona ubutumwa mu sikirini yawe buvuga "ok".
Andika "LED0" hanyuma ukande "ENTER" kugirango uzimye agatara.
Buto yitwa "SPACE" niyo nini muzindi zose kandi iherereye hagati ahagana hasi kucyandikishwa(keyboard).
Andika Andika "LED 1" hanyuma ukande "ENTER".
Haba hari umwanya cyangwa ntauhari hagati ya LED na 1 Ntakibazo.
Andika "LED0" hanyuma ukande "ENTER".
Itara ntirizimye ahubwo ubonye ubutumwa buvuga ngo "Syntax error".
Ntugire ikibazo! Mudasobwa ntijya irakara rwose.
Noneho andika "ABD" ariko ntukande ENTER.
Kanda Buto yitwa "Backspace" kucyandikishwa(keyboard) cyawe.
Buto ya "Backspacr" iri ibururyo bwawe hejuru ku ruhande kucyandikishwa cyawe.
You can delete the character! You don't worry about miss typing.
Gerageza gukora ibi:
1. Andika "ABCDEFG"
2. Gerageza kuzimya itara
3. Gerageza kuzimya watsa itara vuba vuba
4. Andika "BEEP" Hanyuma ukande buto ya "ENTER"
5. Andika "CLS" Hanyuma ukande buto ya "ENTER"
