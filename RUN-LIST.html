Kwibuka porogaramu / RUN/LIST

Reka twibuke porogaramu yo kwatsa agatara kazima kaka.
Iyo utangije umubare biba bigaragazako cyangwa utanze itegeko ryo "kwibuka".
Agatara ntabwo gahise kaka ako kanya.
"run" ni itegeko ryo kuryatsa hanyuma ugakanda buto , Itara rihita ritangira kwaka.
Ahakurikiraho hatangirwa na 20, hanyuma uhongereho muri memori ukanda buto ya ENTER.
Koresha "RUN" Iguhe ushakako porogaramu wabitse itangira gukora.
Itara rizirongera kwaka aruko ongeye kwandika "RUN"!
Ububiko bwite "LIST".
Hagati y'umurongo wa 10 n'uwa 20 ongeramo "WAIT".
Emeza ukoresheje "LIST", yikoreshe ukoresheje "RUN"!
Porogaramu zihuje umubare izibika iyo wanditse nyuma.
(Ukabyemeza ukoresheje "RUN" hamwe na "LIST"!)
Andika umubare wonyine hanyuma ukande buto ya ENTER, ntakintu kizagaragara kuri uwo murongo.
Gerageza!
1. Koresha buto ya "F5" aho gukoresha "RUN"
2. Koresha buto ya "F4" aho gukoresha "LIST"
3. Kora porogaramu yatsa itara inshuro 2
4. Kora porogaramu yatsa itara mu buryo bwiza bwisubiramo.
