Gukuba dukoresheje Umwizenguruko FOR/NEXT(Loop FOR/NEXT) - IchigoJam

Ushobora kubara kuva kuri rimwe kugera kumubare uri muri A wifashishije amabwiriza ya FOR/NEXT. Ubona inyenyeri '*' eshantu(5).
Reka tubihindure dukoreshe imibare ushaka wose, ukoresheje ibwiriza rya INPUT.
Andika umubare ushaka wose!
Ongeraho umubare hanyuma bikore umwizenguruko(Loop)
Ushobora no gukuba imibare!
Shyira umubare mu bubiko C uwo mubare ube '*'.
C=C=A ushobora kubobako igisubizo kirangana na A x '*' kuri buri mwikubo. Noneho reka c=c+1 ibi nabyo ntakibazo. Niba c=0 cyangwa ngo ureke c,o, c ijye yiyongera muburyo bukomeza.
Gerageza gukora ibi bikurikira!
1. Sha igisubizo 7 gukuba 8
2. 8 gukuba 7 bingana bite?
3. Hindura noneho ikimenyetso(Mark) ukoreshe iyo ushakka
4. Noneho hindura mark ige ku nyuguti ebyiri(2)
5. Noneho reba ibyinjira bingana na zeru(0)
