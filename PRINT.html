Gukuba,Guteranya,Gukuramo no Kugabanya imibare - IchigoJam

Ihe ikibazo cyo gushakisha(Gukarikira).
Koresha "PRINT" kugira ngo ugaragaze igisubizo.
Kanda buto ya ENTER, uhuse ubona ko igisubizo ari 「2」.
Kimwe no gukuramo birashoboka!
Kuri mudasobwa(compiyuta), ikimenyetso cyo gukuba kigaragazwa n'inyenyeri "*".
Ikimenyetso cyo kugabanya ni akarongo kaberamye "/". (Ikigabanywa ibumoso, Ikigabanya iburyo)
Muby'ukuri, Ushobora gukoresha akamenyetso ko kubaza '?' aho gukoresha "PRINT".
Oh? Igisubi ntabwo aricyo?
Igisubizo nicyo rwose kuri "IchigoJam" kuberako idakoresha imibare y'ibice.
Kubirango ubone imibare yasigaye inyuma ukoresha '%'.
Kuberako 10 kugabanya 3 ari 3hagasigara 1, uzabona '1' kuko ariyo yasigaye.
Bwanyuma, reka dushake(dukarikire) umubare munini.
Oh!, Yerekanye ibitaribyo.
Mu by'ukuri, IchigoJam ikarikira(Ishakisha) imibare iri hagati yo kuva kuri -32768 no kugeza kuri 32767. Actually,the range that IchigoJam can calculate is determined from -32768 to 32767.

2. Kuba "8*7" Ukoresheje IchigoJam

4. Noneho reba imibare isigara iyo tugabanyije "30/8" muri IchigoJam
