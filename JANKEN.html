Umukino wa Janken

Janken ni umukino w'Abayapani wo kurwanisha ibiganza bakoresheje ibimenyetse bitatu(3) by'ibiganza.
- Gu: (Urutare) Biroroshye funga igipfusi hanyuma ukizamure bwa mbere. (Gu itsinze Choki)
- Choki: (Umukasi) koresha intoki ebyiri(2) izindi uzifunge zikore ishusho nk'iy' Umukasi. (Choki itsinze Pa).
- Pa: (Urupapuro) fungura ikiganza cy'ubamye(mu ikiganza hareba hasi). (Pa itsinzwe Gu)

Koresha itegeko rya random rifite umubare 3 niba dushaka ko guhitamo Gu, Choki cyangwa Pa dukoresheje Mudasobwa.
Urugero: O isobanura Gu, risobanura Choki naho 2 gasobanura Pa.
Andika RUN cyangwa ukande buto ya F5 mu buryo bukurikirana(wongere uyikande)!
Kanda buto ya F5, unavuge ikimenyetse cy'icyiganza cyawe cyo kurwana nyuma yo guhamagara "Jan ken".
Gerageza guca urubanza wifashushuje mudasobwa.
Gerageza!
1. Gerageza kurwana na porogaramu iri muri mudasobwa ya mugenzi wawe(incuti yawe).
2. Gerageza gukora mudasobwa igenda gahoro!!
3. Gerageza gukora umukino wa Janken ugizwe na bakinnyi batatu(3), wowe hamwe n'izindi mudasobwa 2!
