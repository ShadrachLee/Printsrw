Kora imibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) / BIN$

Habaho imibare inyuguti, ingombajwi, utwitso n'ibimenyetso; Ariko mudasobwa y'umva "Zeru(0)" na "Rimwe(1)" gusa.

Ubusanzwe iyo dufashe 1 tugateranyaho 9 bingana ni 10 ahan tuba dufite imibare ibiri zeru iri inyuma ya rimwe. Ariko iyo turi mumibare ya "1" na "0" gusa, iyo dufashe 1 tugateranyaho 1 bingana ni 10 hanyuma tukaba dufite imibarwa ibiri(2). Iyi niyo mibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1).

Itegeko twifashisha muri porogaramu kugirango duhindure imibare isanzwe tuzi muri Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) ni "BIN$".

Ibimenyetso "` " (Dore uko bakandika muri porogaramu: Ukanda Buto ya "shift" + "@" cyangwa ugakanda Kuri buto yegereye "rimwe(1)" ibumoso bwawe kucyandikishwa(keyboard)); Ubwo ni bwo buryo wakwifashisha ukura imibare muri Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) uyishyira mu mibare isanzwe tumenyereye.
0,1,2,3,4, ... gukomeza bifite uko bibawa muri Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1).
Mu Mibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1) tubara "imibarwa".
Ni ukuvuga ngo Gatatu(3) Ni imibarwa ibiri(2) ariyo "11" , Naho 8 ikaba imibarwa ine(4) ariyo "10000".
Gerageza gukora uyu mwitozo!

2. "101","1010","10100" bingana na kangahe mu mibare isanzwe?
3. Noneho "40" ingana gute mu Mibare ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1)?
4. Ese ni uwuhe mubare wanyuma munini ushobora kugira imibarwa itanu(5)?
5. "-1" ingana gute mu Mibarwa ya Zeru(0) na Rimwe(1)?
