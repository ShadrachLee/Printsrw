Kora isaha y'ifashishwa kubara igihe(stopwatch) / TICK

1,2,3,4,5,6...
Ishuro 60 mu gihe kisegonda, komeza kubara igihe, IchigoJam.
Uwo mubare uba ari "TICK". Reka twerekane ibihe byinshi.
Umubare w'ibihe byinshi ni munini ugereranyije n'uwa mbere.
Ugabanyije 60, urahingura amadakika uyashyira mu masegonda.
Itegeko "CLT(clear timer)", Ryifashishwa mukongera gutangira kubara.
Ishobora kumenya igihe mugihe mwayitangije.
Stopwtch ibara igihe mugihe iri gukora "RUN" ukanda buto iri kuri IchigoJam, ku isogonda.
Izategerereza ku murongo wa 20 kugeza igihe uzakandira buto.
Kora stopwatch igaragaza igihe buri uko ukanze buto.
Gerageza!
1. Reka tubare igihe ushobora kumara uri kwayura.
2. Ufunga amaso, hanyuma ukande buto ibare amasegonda 5 mbere yiyafungura.
3. Siba "/60" kumurongo wa 30 hanyuma ubare umufuduko uri gukoresha ukanda buto.
4. Bika porogaramu wifashishje "SAVE0", hanyuma zimya, wongere ufungure IchigoJam mugihe ugikanze buto, irahita itangira ako kanya, noneho kanda buto burikanya!
5.Noneho bihindure kuburyo uzajya utangira ukanda buto hanyuma ikaragaza amasegonda.
