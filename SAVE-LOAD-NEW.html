Bika porogaramu / Bika/Tangiza/shyashya

Porogaramu yo kwatsa itara. kugirango ubike porogaramu wifashisha "SAVE".
Mugihe ubonye "OK", porogaramu ibayemaze kubikwa.
IchigoJam ishobora kubika porogaramu zigera kuri enye(4). Ni ukuvuga 0-3
Funga ufungure IchigoJam hanyuma hanyuma ukore load(gutangiza) porogaramu wifashishije "LOAD".
Ubyemeze ukoresheje "LIST". Uyikoreshe ukoresheje "RUN"!
"FILES" igufasha kumenya porogaramu umaze kubika.
Witonda hatagira ikosa ukora ugasiba porogaramu.
"NEW" igufasha kwandika(gukora) porogaramu shyashya.
Andika "LIST", Ntakintu nakimwe kigaragara.
Niba nta porogaramu bitse. ukimara gufungura uzahita ubona 0 mu gihe ukanze buto yo kuri IchigoJam.
(Ntakibazo niba udafit TV(televiziyo) cyangwa Keyboard. ibindi bikoresho bya elegitolonike birakora hamwe nibindi.)
Gerageza!
1. Bika porogaramu yatsa itara inshuro 3
2. Koresha ubukryo bwo gutangira yikoresha.
3. Reba ibiba igihe ukoresheje "SAVE" nyuma ya "NEW"
